2023 Suicide Data — CDC
14.12 per 100,000 people died by suicide in 2023.
Firearms accounted for 55.36% of all suicide deaths.
Men died by suicide about 3.9× more than women.
White males accounted for 68.13% of suicide deaths.
An estimated 1.6 million suicide attempts were reported for 2022 — 2023 finalized data is pending.

As responsible gun owners we have a duty to take mental health seriously — for ourselves, our families, and our communities. If you or someone you know is struggling, these resources are here to help.
